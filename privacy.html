Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Data We Collect

RigRadar AI does not collect any personal information that directly identifies you (such as name or address) unless you provide it to us yourself. We only store:

  • Search queries (what you are looking for).
  • Technical device data (IP address, browser type) for security and attack prevention purposes.

2. How We Use AI

Your search queries are processed using the Google Gemini API for price analysis. Data is transmitted anonymously and serves only to generate a "value score" and commentary.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising. We use your browser's Local Storage to save your search history so you can see it on your next visit. This data remains on your device.

4. Third-Party Services

To operate the application, we use the following services:

  • Vercel & Render: Hosting and backend infrastructure.
  • ScraperAPI: To obtain public data from e-shops.
  • Google Gemini: For AI text analysis.
